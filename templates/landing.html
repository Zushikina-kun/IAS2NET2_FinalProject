The Process
Walk in. Get recognized. Done.

From face to access in under a second — completely automatic.

01
Approach Camera
Member walks up to the gym entrance where the webcam is positioned.
02
Face Detected
The system spots their face instantly and starts the identification process.
03
Identity Verified
AI matches their face against the member database with high accuracy.
04
Membership Checked
System confirms their membership is active and not expired or frozen.
05
Access Granted
Green light — member walks in. Entry logged automatically. Gym floor updated.
06
Everything Logged
Time, name, emotion, and access result all recorded for analytics and audit.
