❓ Frequently Asked Questions
How do I add a new gym member? ▶
Go to Members → + Add Member. Fill in their details (name, membership type, dates, contact info). After saving, the camera will open to capture 50 face photos for recognition.
The system doesn't recognize a member's face ▶
Go to their profile and click "Add More Photos" to capture additional face images. Make sure they face the camera directly in good lighting. More photos = better accuracy. You can also run normalize_dataset.py to enhance all existing photos.
How does the access control work? ▶
When someone stands in front of the camera, the system: (1) detects their face, (2) identifies them against the dataset, (3) checks their membership status. If they're an active member with a valid (non-expired) membership, access is GRANTED. Otherwise, it's DENIED.
What's the difference between Expired and Suspended? ▶
Expired means their membership period ended (date passed). Suspended means an admin manually paused their access (e.g., for rule violations). Both result in denied entry. Use "Renew" to reactivate expired members, or "Suspend" toggle for manual control.
How do I renew a membership? ▶
On the Members page, expired/suspended members show a Renew button. Clicking it extends their membership by their plan duration (1 month, 3 months, or 1 year) from today or their current expiry date, whichever is later.
Can I have multiple admin accounts? ▶
Yes! Go to Admin Accounts (superadmin only). You can create staff accounts with limited access or additional superadmin accounts. Staff can manage members and view logs. Superadmins can also manage other admin accounts.
How do I back up my data? ▶
Go to Backups and click "Create Backup". This saves a timestamped copy of all member data and entry logs. You can restore from any previous backup if something goes wrong.
The camera shows "initializing" and never loads ▶
Another application is using the webcam, or a stale Python process is running. Close all other camera apps, then kill stale processes: open Task Manager → End all Python processes → restart the server.
I'm locked out after too many login attempts ▶
The system locks your IP for 5 minutes after 5 failed attempts. Wait for the lockout to expire, or restart the server to reset all rate limits.
How do I export entry logs? ▶
Go to Entry Log, optionally filter by date, then click the "Export CSV" button. The file downloads immediately with all matching records.
How do I record a payment? ▶
Go to Payments → Record Payment. Select the member, enter the amount, choose the payment method (cash, GCash, card, bank transfer), and add a description. All payments are logged with who recorded them and when.
What does "Freeze" do? ▶
Freezing pauses a membership — the expiry countdown stops. When you unfreeze, the expiry date is extended by the number of days it was frozen. Use this for medical leave, vacations, or temporary holds. Frozen members are denied entry.
What is the Gym Floor page? ▶
The Gym Floor shows who is currently inside the gym in real-time. Members are automatically checked in when face recognition grants them access. Staff can manually check out members when they leave. The count resets when the server restarts.
What does the Audit Log track? ▶
The audit log records every admin action: adding/deleting members, suspending/freezing/renewing memberships, recording payments, creating backups, and restoring data. It shows who did what and when. Only superadmins can view it.
🔐 Admin Roles
Superadmin
Full access: manage members, logs, analytics, backups, AND create/delete other admin accounts
Staff
Can manage members, view entry logs, analytics, and backups. Cannot manage admin accounts.
