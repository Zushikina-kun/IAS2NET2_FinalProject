What It Does
Everything a modern gym needs

From AI face recognition to payment tracking and real-time analytics — all in one self-contained system.

🤖
AI Face Recognition
VGG-Face deep learning model with cosine distance matching. Identifies members in real time from a live webcam feed.
🔐
Instant Access Control
Grants or denies entry based on live membership status. Active members get in — expired, suspended, or frozen members are blocked.
😊
Emotion & Demographics
DeepFace detects emotion (happy, sad, angry, neutral…), estimates gender and age — all overlaid live on the camera feed.
👥
Full Member Management
Add, edit, suspend, freeze, renew, or delete members. Track visits, store contact info, emergency contacts, and notes.
💰
Payment Tracking
Record payments via cash, GCash, card, or bank transfer. View per-member history and total revenue at a glance.
📊
Live Analytics
Daily attendance charts, hourly distribution, top visitors, emotion breakdowns, and peak hour detection — all auto-generated.
🏠
Gym Floor Monitor
Real-time occupancy view showing exactly who is currently inside the gym. Members auto check-in on face recognition.
🔑
Multi-Admin & Roles
Superadmin and Staff roles with separate permissions. Rate-limited login, session timeout, SHA-256 password hashing.
💾
Backup & Restore
One-click timestamped data snapshots. Restore any backup instantly. Full audit trail of every admin action.
The Process
How it works

Six steps from face to access — all happening in under a second.

01
Face Detected
OpenCV Haar Cascade detects a face in the webcam frame (min 100×100 px, minNeighbors=6).
02
Crop & Enhance
Largest face is cropped, resized to 224×224, and CLAHE contrast enhancement is applied.
03
Identity Match
DeepFace.find() compares against the member dataset using cosine distance ≤ 0.40.
04
Status Check
Membership status is verified in real time — active, expired, suspended, or frozen.
05
Analysis
DeepFace.analyze() detects emotion, estimates gender and age from the face.
06
Access Granted
Result shown on overlay. Entry logged to CSV. Member auto checked into Gym Floor.
